Modeling Diseases Globally with Arima

Out of models tested we found Arima to be closest to true value in its predictions.
Arima offers the average of a moving window as a prediction. This makes it more useful for short term predictions, as for longer periods predictions stay a constant number.
By iteratively refitting data with new observations of case counts accuracy was maintained throughout.
